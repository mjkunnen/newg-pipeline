## NEWGARMENTS Strategic Insights

### 1. HEAVYWEIGHT/QUALITY = OPEN WHITESPACE
Only **2 out of 118 hooks** lead with quality/material. Most competitors use identity/style hooks. NG should lead with fabric weight, GSM closeups, and "built to last" messaging. This is your biggest competitive gap.

### 2. SCARCITY IS UNDERUSED
Only **4 hooks** use scarcity/urgency. NG's real "no restock" model is a genuine differentiator vs competitors who fake it.

### 3. REGION OPPORTUNITY
US is saturated (138 ads). **UK (12), Germany (12), France (8), Netherlands (2)** have much less competition. Target EU harder.

### 4. CTA DIFFERENTIATION
"Shop now" dominates (106 ads). NG should use urgency CTAs: **"Cop before it's gone"**, **"Secure yours"**, **"Join the archive"**.

### 5. HOOKS TO ADAPT FOR NG
- "the perfect blank hoodie under $100" -> **"the heavyweight hoodie they'll ask about"**
- "POV: you found the HEAVYWEIGHT hoodie of your dreams" -> **"POV: archive streetwear that never restocks"**
- "hardest tee in the market" -> **"hardest drop this year. 200 pieces. gone forever."**
- "Would you wear this tee?" -> **"only 3% will cop this before it's gone"**

### 6. PROVEN AD FORMATS
**51 streetwear ads** running 30+ days = validated by real spend. Study their video format, not just copy.
