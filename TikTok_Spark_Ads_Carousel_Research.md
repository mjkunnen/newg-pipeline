## 1. HOW SPARK ADS WORK WITH CAROUSEL/PHOTO POSTS

### Format Distinction: Two Different Systems
There are TWO distinct carousel-related ad formats on TikTok, and they work differently:

**A) Standard Carousel Ads (Ads Manager native)**
- Created directly in TikTok Ads Manager
- 2-10 image cards per ad
- Each card can link to a unique landing page
- Appears in the For You Page / News Feed
- Supports Traffic, Conversions, and Reach objectives
- Spark Ads ARE supported for Standard Carousel format
- No music/audio on carousel ads (unlike organic photo posts)

**B) Organic Photo Mode Posts (boosted as Spark Ads)**
- Created organically through TikTok's photo upload flow
- Supports up to 35 images
- Includes background music (required)
- Can be boosted as a Spark Ad
- Algorithm interprets manual swiping as high-engagement signal
- TikTok indexes text overlays on individual slides for search
- NOTE: Some sub-formats (Story, duo carousel) are NOT compatible with Spark Ads

### Key Mechanic: How Spark Ads + Carousel Works
When you boost an organic carousel post as a Spark Ad:
- All engagement (views, comments, shares, likes, follows) is attributed back to the organic post
- The ad appears native in the feed, looking identical to organic content
- You retain the original post's social proof (existing likes/comments)
- The Spark Ad can be created by selecting "+Add videos or images" in the creative drawer

### Specs for Carousel Ads
- Image file types: JPG, JPEG, PNG
- Recommended resolution: 1080x1920px (9:16 vertical) for best native feel
- Alternative ratios: 1200x628 (horizontal), 640x640 (square), 720x1280 (vertical)
- Max file size per image: 100KB recommended for optimal loading
- Total carousel file size: 500MB max
- Caption: 20 characters for "Ad Caption" field, 40 characters for "Ad Title" (varies by placement)
- Key implication: Put important text IN the images as overlays, not just in the caption field

### Spark Ads Specific Results
- New profile landing page UI: 69% higher conversion rate, 37% lower CPA

### Why Spark Ads Win on Conversion
Spark Ads look organic. Users engage with them as if they were normal posts. This "native feel" is the #1 driver of their superior performance. The social proof (existing likes/comments on the organic post) creates trust and reduces purchase hesitation.

## 4. SLIDE ORDER & FIRST SLIDE IMPORTANCE

### First Slide = Make or Break
The first slide serves as the "hook" that determines whether viewers swipe through the rest. If the first image isn't a "scroll stopper," the entire carousel fails regardless of subsequent quality.

### Three Types of First-Slide Hooks
1. **Text Hook**: Keywords shown as on-screen text or overlays (e.g., "The dress everyone's asking about")
2. **Visual Hook**: Product displayed immediately and clearly with striking imagery
3. **Value Hook**: Problem statement or value proposition introduced early (e.g., "Finally, jeans that fit short girls")

### Slide Sequencing Best Practices
- **Slide 1**: Hook/scroll-stopper - bold visual, compelling question, or surprising stat
- **Slides 2-4**: Build the story - show angles, colors, use cases, styling options
- **Final slide**: Clear CTA or "reveal" moment
- For fashion: Show different angles, colors, or styling contexts across slides
- Treat the carousel as a cohesive narrative, not a random collection

### Paid vs. Organic: First Slide Dynamics
- In organic: The first slide determines whether the algorithm surfaces the post at all
- In paid: The first slide determines whether users engage (stopping the scroll) despite guaranteed impressions
- TikTok's Smart Creative AI can automatically test different image orders and prioritize combinations that drive the most conversions
- Recommendation: Let Smart Creative test slide order, but always ensure your strongest visual is among the first 2 slides

### Smart Creative AI Optimization
TikTok's system automatically tests:
- Different image orders
- Different caption variations
- Different CTA combinations
Then it prioritizes the winning combinations. This means you should provide multiple strong images and let the system optimize, rather than rigidly fixing slide order.

## 5. CREATIVE STYLE: WHAT CONVERTS FOR FASHION

### UGC/Native Style vs. Polished Production
**The data is clear**: UGC-style content outperforms polished brand creative on TikTok.

- Ads that look like UGC convert **40% better** than polished brand commercials
- Native, creator-style content beats polished ads in both CTR and share rate
- Spark Ads and UGC-style videos deliver higher CVR than polished studio ads
- Gap's raw creator footage campaign: 400M views in 3 days

**Exception**: Polished creative can win when a product's features need clear demonstration or brand positioning demands a premium look. But even then, it should feel "TikTok-native" rather than "TV commercial."

### Lifestyle vs. Product Shots vs. Mixed
**Mixed/combination content performs best.** Neither pure product shots nor pure lifestyle imagery wins alone.

Key findings:
- "Customers want to see how pieces look in the real world, not in a studio"
- Generic lifestyle shots AND sterile product photos are both ineffective
- Best approach: Authentic, real-world scenarios WITH clear product visibility
- Creator-led videos incorporating products into daily routines + professionally-shot lifestyle campaigns = optimal mix
- For fashion specifically: Try-ons, GRWM, OOTD, hauls, and styling tips outperform everything else

### Top-Performing Fashion Ad Formats
1. **Try-on / styling videos** (adapted as carousel = outfit progression)
2. **GRWM (Get Ready With Me)** sequences
3. **OOTD (Outfit of the Day)** slideshows
4. **Haul-style** product showcases
5. **Review / social proof** carousels
6. **Before/after** styling transformations

### Production Tips That Drive Conversion
- Full-screen (9:16) content gets up to 40% more views
- Sound-on content drives 2x more add-to-carts
- Hook within first 3 seconds is critical
- Feature real customers/creators in different sizes, colors, and fits
- Shoot in natural settings (bedrooms, hallways, streets) - NOT studios
- The best fashion ads "look nothing like ads"

## 6. CAPTION STRATEGY: DOES IT MATTER IN PAID?

### Yes, Captions Matter Significantly - Even in Paid

**TikTok's own data on text/caption impact:**
- +58% uplift in ad recall
- +25% bump in perceived uniqueness
- +95% surge in brand affinity
- +31% improvement in likeability
- Title text at start of video: +48% brand recall, +11% purchase intent

**Optimal text pacing**: 5-10 words per second = +2.1x awareness lift vs other timings

### Native-Sounding vs. Marketing-Style Captions
The data consistently shows native-feeling captions outperform marketing copy:
- Casual, conversational tone aligns with platform culture
- "Buzzfeed-esque" hooks work well (lists, questions, relatability)
- UGC-style captions (as if a real person wrote it) drive better engagement
- Marketing jargon reduces native feel and hurts performance

### Caption Best Practices for Conversion
1. **Keep it short**: The ad caption field is limited (20-40 chars depending on placement), so brevity is mandatory
2. **Put key info IN images**: Text overlays on slides are more impactful than the caption field
3. **Hook in text overlay on slide 1**: This is what actually stops the scroll
4. **Include clear CTA**: Whether in overlay or caption, make the action obvious
5. **Don't over-brand**: Subtle product mentions outperform heavy branding
6. **Mention the product benefit, not just the name**: "These jeans stretch 4 ways" > "Shop Brand X Jeans"

### Caption Approaches That Work for Fashion Carousels
- "The [product] that broke TikTok"
- "POV: you finally found [solution to common problem]"
- "Okay but why did nobody tell me about these"
- "[Number] ways to style [product]"
- "The dress for girls who hate dresses"
- Question-based: "Would you wear this to [occasion]?"

## 7. TARGETING & CREATIVE STYLE INTERACTION

### Broad Targeting + Strong Creative = Best Results
TikTok's own data shows:
- Broad audiences (80%+ of potential users) achieve **15% lower CPA** and **20% higher conversion rates** vs. narrow targeting
- The algorithm in 2025-2026 is sophisticated enough to find converters within broad audiences
- Start with audiences of 10M+ for optimal algorithm learning

### Targeting Layers for Fashion
1. **Broad targeting** (recommended starting point): Let TikTok's algorithm find your buyers
2. **Interest targeting**: Fashion, beauty, shopping (passive, long-term preferences)
3. **Behavior targeting**: Recent interactions with fashion content (active, high-intent - last 7-15 days)
4. **Lookalike audiences**: Based on existing customers (minimum 1,000-10,000 source users)

### How Creative Style Interacts with Targeting
- **Broad targeting + native/UGC creative**: Best combination for discovery and scale
- **Behavior targeting + product-focused creative**: Best for high-intent audiences near purchase
- **Interest targeting + lifestyle creative**: Good for awareness and consideration
- **Lookalike + mixed creative**: Effective for scaling proven audiences

### Key Insight
Behavior targeting outperforms interest targeting for conversion campaigns. Interests are passive ("I like fashion"), while behaviors are active and recent ("I watched 5 fashion videos to completion in the last week").

## 8. ACTIONABLE RECOMMENDATIONS

### For Running Spark Ads on TikTok Carousels/Slideshows:

1. **Create organic photo-mode posts first** - Use TikTok's native photo upload (not video slideshow). Post them organically and let them gather initial engagement before boosting.

2. **Boost winners as Spark Ads** - Identify which organic carousels get the best engagement, then boost those. The Soli case study proves this approach: adapting successful organic carousels drove 10x+ conversions.

3. **Use 5-10 slides** - This is the sweet spot. Fewer feels thin, more causes drop-off.

4. **First slide must stop the scroll** - Use a text hook, visual hook, or value hook. This is non-negotiable for both organic and paid.

5. **Enable Smart Creative** - Let TikTok's AI test slide order, caption, and CTA combinations for optimal conversion.

6. **Go native, not polished** - UGC-style converts 40% better. Shoot in real environments, use real people, keep it authentic.

7. **Mixed content wins** - Combine lifestyle context WITH clear product visibility. Pure product shots and pure lifestyle both underperform mixed approaches.

8. **Caption: short, native, benefit-focused** - Use casual tone, mention the product benefit (not just brand name), include a CTA. Put key text IN the image overlays, not just the caption field.

9. **Start with broad targeting** - 15% lower CPA than narrow targeting. Let the algorithm work.

10. **Test volume matters** - Advertisers with multiple ad variations see 17% higher CVR. Test 10-20 creatives weekly.

### Expected Performance Benchmarks for Fashion Carousel Spark Ads:
- CTR: Target 0.8-1.5% (above fashion average, achievable with carousel + Spark)
- CVR: Target 1.5-3% (above average with optimized creative)
- ROAS: Target 3-5x (above fashion average of 3.5x)
- CPA: Expect 30-40% lower than standard in-feed ads
