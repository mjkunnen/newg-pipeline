## 1. PLATFORM-LEVEL CAROUSEL DATA

### Why Carousels Outperform Video on TikTok (2025-2026)

TikTok has been actively promoting carousel/photo mode content since late 2024. In early 2025 they rolled out a dedicated photo feed and added alt-text support for images, signaling algorithmic preference for the format.

**Hard numbers:**
- Photo carousels generate **81% more engagements** and **82% more likes** than videos on TikTok (2025 data analysis)
- Carousels achieve approximately **3% higher reach on average** than video posts
- TikTok's own internal data: carousels get **2.9x more comments**, **1.9x more likes**, and **2.6x more shares** than video posts
- Save rate benchmark: above **3% is strong**, above **5% is exceptional** for carousels
- Comment rate on carousels typically runs **above 0.5%** (higher than video benchmark)

**Why the algorithm favors them:**
- TikTok ranks carousels by **swipe-through rate**, **dwell time**, and **reverse swipes** (not watch time like video)
- Each swipe is an engagement action the algorithm registers positively
- Extended dwell time from swiping signals high-value content
- Viewers pause, swipe, and re-read slides -- all strong engagement signals that boost distribution

**Optimal slide count:** 4-8 slides is the sweet spot. Long enough to tell a story and build dwell time, but not so long you risk low completion rate.

**Maximum capacity:** Up to 35 photos per carousel post.

**Technical specs:** 1080x1920 pixels (9:16 vertical), consistent color schemes, high-resolution images.

## 3. VIRAL SLIDESHOW FORMAT PATTERNS

### Top-Performing Carousel/Slideshow Formats for Streetwear

Based on analysis across all brands researched:

#### FORMAT 1: "Before/After Transformation"
- Loading screen between "before" and "after" images creates suspense
- Leverages the natural tension of the reveal
- Deeply shareable because of surprise element
- Example: Showing raw fabric/production to finished product

#### FORMAT 2: "Product Collage / Flat Lay Grid"
- Multiple product shots arranged aesthetically
- Each slide shows a different outfit or colorway
- Works especially well for drops with multiple pieces
- Example: Femmenina's flat-lay presentations on ruffled bedspreads -- lets viewers appreciate intricate details without distraction

#### FORMAT 3: "Outfit of the Day / Fit Check Slideshow"
- Multiple angles of the same outfit
- Street/urban setting photography
- Text overlay with item names and prices
- 4-6 slides per post optimal

#### FORMAT 4: "Collection Reveal / Lookbook"
- Each slide reveals a new piece from a collection
- Builds anticipation through sequential reveal
- Strong hook on slide 1 creates curiosity gap
- Example: Cole Buxton x ComplexCon collection reveal

#### FORMAT 5: "Brand Story / Origin Journey"
- Slideshow telling the brand's founding story
- Works exceptionally well for small brands (Unknown London: "from shed to global brand")
- Relatable entrepreneurial narrative
- Drives saves and shares

#### FORMAT 6: "Quick-Cut Product Color/Style Variants"
- Ralph Lauren's approach: rapidly showcasing a product in different colors/styling contexts
- Almost hypnotic effect through fast-paced editing
- Shows versatility of a single product
- Translates well to carousel format with one colorway per slide

#### FORMAT 7: "Ranking / Listicle"
- "Top 5 [brand] pieces from the collection"
- "Best streetwear brands you don't know about"
- Each list item gets its own slide
- Viewers feel compelled to swipe to see all items
- "Save for later" impulse is extremely strong
- Undisputed king format for carousel engagement

#### FORMAT 8: "Indecision / Opinion Loop"
- Show multiple similar items, declare each one the "favorite" then change your mind
- Pattern interrupt generates curiosity about the final choice
- Drives comments ("which one did you pick?!")
- Example: Odd Muse's three-dress video hit 1.4M+ views

#### FORMAT 9: "Vox Pop / Street Interview"
- Creator interviews random people about what they're wearing
- #voxpop has over 240 million views on TikTok
- When combined with streetwear brands, drives millions of views
- Naturally leads to brand mentions and product exposure
- Can be adapted as a carousel with still photos of each person's outfit

### Key Technical Best Practices

| Element | Best Practice |
|---------|--------------|
| **Slide 1** | Must grab attention and create a curiosity gap |
| **Text on slides** | Now indexed by TikTok -- use clear, readable fonts with strategic keywords |
| **Captions** | At least 200 characters long for searchability |
| **Music** | 80% of top viral content uses upbeat music; sync transitions to beats |
| **Filters/effects** | Match theme and brand identity; maintain consistency |
| **Color scheme** | Consistent across all slides for polished look |
| **Resolution** | 1080x1920 minimum |
| **Slide count** | 4-8 for engagement sweet spot |

## 5. ACTIONABLE TAKEAWAYS FOR NEWGARMENTS

### Immediate Actions

1. **Start posting carousels/photo mode content immediately.** TikTok is actively boosting this format with 81% more engagements and 3% higher reach than video. This is the single biggest algorithmic opportunity right now.

2. **Adopt the 4-8 slide sweet spot.** Every carousel should tell a mini-story: Hook (slide 1) > Value/reveal (slides 2-6) > CTA or payoff (final slide).

3. **Use text overlays strategically on slides.** TikTok now indexes text on carousel images -- place product names, collection names, and relevant keywords directly on slides.

4. **Write captions of 200+ characters.** This is TikTok's own recommendation for making carousels searchable and discoverable.

### Content Formats to Replicate

| Priority | Format | Why | Example |
|----------|--------|-----|---------|
| **#1** | Product collection reveal carousel | Builds anticipation, high swipe-through | "New drop: 6 pieces, which is your favorite?" |
| **#2** | Flat-lay outfit grid slideshow | Clean aesthetic, high saves | Product laid flat on branded background, one outfit per slide |
| **#3** | Brand origin story carousel | Unknown London proved this works | "From [X] to shipping worldwide" in 5 slides |
| **#4** | Ranking/listicle carousel | Highest save rate format | "Top 5 pieces from our winter collection" |
| **#5** | Before/after production reveal | Factory-to-finished product | Raw materials > cutting > sewing > finished hoodie |
| **#6** | Fit check multi-angle slideshow | Street photography, authentic feel | Same outfit from 4 angles in urban setting |

### Paid Strategy

5. **Run Spark Ads on best-performing organic carousels.** Represent Clo proved this works with GBP 4.30 ROAS. HUH Clothing got 2M views in 3 months. The key: amplify content that already performs organically.

6. **Use Lead Generation objective first, not direct conversion.** Both Represent and HUH saw better results with lead gen. Represent's 14% lead-to-purchase conversion rate proves the funnel works.

7. **Target UK + EU + USA markets** following Represent Clo's expansion playbook.

### Content Authenticity Rules

8. **Keep content unpolished and native-feeling.** HUH Clothing's success was built on "honest, everyday content that felt real." Highly edited brand videos underperform.

9. **Enable UGC amplification.** Corteiz and Broken Planet prove that the most powerful content comes from fans, not the brand. Create moments worth sharing (drops, events, limited pieces).

10. **Post 3-4 times per week minimum.** Successful TikTok brands average 3.52 posts per week. Brands generating ~GBP 1M monthly on TikTok Shop produce 1,000+ videos monthly.

### Scarcity and Drop Culture

11. **Leverage scarcity psychology in carousel content.** Corteiz's entire model is built on limited drops that sell out in seconds. Even a slideshow announcing "only 50 made" drives urgency.

12. **Document the drop process in carousels.** Production photos > warehouse packing > shipping = compelling 6-slide story that builds community investment.
