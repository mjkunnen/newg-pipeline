## KEY INSIGHTS FOR NEWGARMENTS

1. **Cargo pants are the #1 winner** — 46 stores, 142 mentions. Multiple single-product stores exist just for cargo pants. This is THE proven dropship product in streetwear right now.

2. **Oversized tees (anime + vintage wash) are #2** — Combined 64 mentions across anime and non-anime variants. Tokyo-Tiger alone runs 172 ads on washed tees.

3. **Dear-Lover is not a competitor — it's a supplier.** They have a Shopify dropshipping app, US warehouse, and serve 410K+ retailers. Worth investigating as a supply partner.

4. **"The Comfy Cargo Pants" is a textbook single-product dropship store** with terrible Trustpilot reviews (wrong sizes, China shipping, thin material). There's room to do this BETTER with quality product.

5. **Anime/pop culture tees have legal risk** but massive demand. Consider original streetwear graphics inspired by anime aesthetics rather than direct character prints.

6. **Price sweet spot:** Most dropshippers price at $25-45 for pants, $20-35 for tees. Cost from China is $5-18 depending on product. Margins are 50-70%.
