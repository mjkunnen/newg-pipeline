## 5. TIKTOK CAROUSEL/SLIDESHOW BEST PRACTICES (DATA-BACKED)

### Performance Statistics
| Metric | Carousel vs. Video Performance |
|--------|-------------------------------|
| Comments | 2.9x more than standard videos |
| Likes | 1.9x more than standard videos |
| Shares | 2.6x more than standard videos |
| Views | 11% higher reach (Youthforia case study) |
| Product Clicks | 25% more clicks (fashion brand micro-test) |

*Source: TikTok official data (Feb 2024), Social Insider, Buffer*

### Technical Specifications
- **Max slides:** 35 images per carousel
- **Optimal slides:** 5-15 for high performance
- **Image format:** PNG or JPG only
- **Dimensions:** 1080 x 1920 pixels (9:16 portrait)
- **Aspect ratios supported:** 9:16, 1:1, 4:5
- **Duration per slide:** 2-4 seconds recommended
- **Total slideshow length:** 15-30 seconds optimal

### Slide Design Strategy

**Slide 1 (The Hook):**
- Bold headline with high-contrast visuals
- Scroll-stopping image or intriguing question
- Striking clothing shot or dramatic reveal setup
- This determines whether users swipe or scroll past

**Middle Slides (The Story):**
- Consistent color palette and fonts across all slides
- 1-3 lines of text per slide for mobile readability
- Show multiple product angles, color variants, or styling options
- Each slide should contribute meaningfully to the narrative

**Final Slide (The CTA):**
- Clear call-to-action (follow, check bio, comment)
- Can include price point or availability info
- Should create urgency or curiosity

### Text Overlay Strategy
- Text overlays are INDEXED by TikTok's search engine
- Place target keywords in slide text for TikTok SEO
- Keep text large enough to read on mobile
- Overlays should "enhance rather than overwhelm" visuals
- Use consistent brand fonts across all slides
- Numbered slide indicators (e.g., "1/8") signal more content and encourage swiping

### Music & Audio Strategy
- Trending sounds dramatically increase algorithmic reach (content appears in sound's feed)
- TikTok auto-selects trending audio for carousels (can be customized)
- All carousels require sound; silence achieved by reducing volume to zero
- Music should emotionally complement the visual narrative
- Currently trending: "That's my life" bridge cuts for highlight-style content
- "Primadonna Girl" (Marina) trending for before/after carousel content

### Caption Strategy
- Longer captions (up to 2,200 characters) receive 3x more views on average
- Include compelling hooks in caption text
- Use relevant hashtags for discoverability
- Ask questions or use CTAs to drive comments
- Caption should add context beyond what slides show

### Posting Strategy
- 3-5 times per week for consistency
- Respond to comments quickly to boost interaction rates
- Track saves and shares as quality indicators
- Cross-promote carousel snippets on Instagram Stories

## 6. RECOMMENDED CAROUSEL FORMATS FOR STREETWEAR BRANDS

Based on research across all sources, these 7 carousel types perform best:

### 1. "Where Is This Going?" Post
Slide 1 creates curiosity, each subsequent slide builds suspense, final slide delivers the payoff. Great for product reveals and drop announcements.

### 2. Themed Collection Post
Showcase an entire collection or drop across slides. Each slide = one piece, consistent styling/backdrop. Works well for new season launches.

### 3. Trend Post
Ride a trending format (currently "Primadonna Girl," "In a World Full of...") but adapt it to showcase products. Borrows existing audience from trending sounds.

### 4. One-Two Punch Post
Slide 1: Setup (e.g., "what I ordered"). Slide 2: Payoff (e.g., "what arrived"). Simple but highly shareable. Good for quality flex content.

### 5. Before/After Transformation
Show outfit evolution, styling progression, or brand growth story. Each slide marks a stage. High save rate format.

### 6. Product Showcase Carousel
Multiple angles, color variants, size guides, or use-case scenarios in one post. Drives "significantly higher purchase intent than single-image ads."

### 7. Social Proof / UGC Carousel
Compile customer photos, reviews, or fit checks into one carousel. Builds trust and community. High comment engagement.

## 7. KEY STRATEGIC TAKEAWAYS FOR NEWGARMENTS

1. **Carousel > Video for engagement:** TikTok's own data shows 2.9x more comments, 1.9x more likes, 2.6x more shares vs. standard video

2. **Gymshark's full pivot proves the model:** A 5M-follower account went 100% carousel and maintained/grew engagement

3. **Five Leafs' playbook is working:** Their primary customer acquisition channel is TikTok, with Trustpilot reviews confirming "found them on TikTok" as common discovery path

4. **Text overlays = TikTok SEO:** Every text overlay is indexed by search. Use keywords like "streetwear drop," "premium hoodie," "oversized fit" in slide text

5. **5-10 slides is the sweet spot:** Enough for storytelling, not so many that users abandon

6. **Hook slide is everything:** Bold, high-contrast, curiosity-driven first image determines carousel success

7. **Trending audio multiplies reach:** Carousels using trending sounds appear in that sound's feed, dramatically expanding organic reach

8. **European streetwear is booming on TikTok:** Nude Project, Eme Studios, Corteiz, Five Leafs all proving the EU market is receptive to TikTok-first streetwear brands
