## THE RESEARCH BEHIND THIS

### Why Simple Captions Win (Data)

- Spark Ads with native captions: **25-40% lower CPAs** (TikTok internal data)
- Native captions: **69% higher conversion rate**, **37% lower CPA** vs marketing copy
- Creator-led native content: **70% higher CTR**, **159% higher engagement** (TikTok 2026)
- Mobile-shot content: **63% chance of outperforming** studio-shot for purchases (TikTok official)
- UGC-style ads convert **40% better** than polished brand creative

### Why "School Fit" > "460 GSM Heavyweight Hoodie" (Psychology)

1. **Processing fluency** (Journal of Interactive Advertising): Simple = perceived more positively
2. **Cognitive load**: Social media already uses working memory. Adding specs = overload = scroll past
3. **Identity signaling**: "School fit" = "this is WHO I AM." "460 GSM" = "this is WHAT the product IS." TikTok users scroll for identity, not education
4. **Ad blindness**: 66% of consumers prefer friend/creator recommendations over polished ads. Marketing-speak triggers ad detection
5. **The save loop**: Simple utility captions trigger saves. Saves = #1 algorithm signal. Nobody saves "460 GSM comparison"

### Caption Locked in Spark Ads (Critical Technical Detail)

When you boost an organic post as a Spark Ad, the caption **CANNOT be edited**. It carries over from the original post. This means:
- Your organic caption IS your ad copy
- Simple native captions work in BOTH contexts (organic + paid)
- Marketing captions that feel wrong in organic feed ALSO feel wrong as ads

### Caption Category 1: Identity/Situation (Highest Virality)

**1. "School fit"** — The #1 performer. Product as part of daily life, not a sales pitch.
**2. "Fit check"** — TikTok's most native fashion format. Mirror selfies, real settings.
**3. "Main character fit"** — Taps into Gen Z's deepest aspiration: standing out.

**4. "Let me save your fashion style"** — Positions you as helper, not seller. Triggers bookmark behavior.

**6. "Which one are you? 1, 2, or 3?"** — Low-friction choice. Every comment = Spark Ad fuel.

**8. "Rate this fit 1-10"** — Numbers are the easiest engagement. Gamified interaction.
**9. "This or that?"** — Comparison format perfect for carousels. Drives debate.

### Caption Category 4: Seasonal/Occasion (Timing Relevance)

**10. "Winter fit"** — Rotate through seasons. Meet them where they are mentally.

### Caption Category 5: Aspirational/Permission (Conversion Push)

**11. "POV: you actually have style"** — Flattery + aspiration. Native TikTok format.
**12. "Your sign to finally cop this"** — Permission framing for people already considering purchase.

## SPARK ADS WORKFLOW

### The Pipeline (Fashion Nova Formula — 40% Lower CPA)

1. **Shoot**: Create 10-15 slideshow variations per week (different people, settings, captions)
2. **Post organically**: Upload with simple caption + trending sound
3. **Monitor 24-48 hours**: Track saves, comments, shares
4. **Boost top 2-3**: Promote winners as Spark Ads with conversion objective
5. **Iterate weekly**: Pause underperformers. Refresh creative. New round.

### Volume Rules

- **10-15 new slideshows per week** — creative fatigue hits after 3-5 days
- **5+ variations see 2.3x better ROAS** than single-creative approaches
- Same hoodie, different: people, settings, outfits, captions, colorways
- Let TikTok Smart Creative optimize slide order within each post

### Caption Rotation

Rotate through caption categories to avoid fatigue:
- Mon: Identity ("School fit")
- Tue: Save ("Let me save your style")
- Wed: Question ("Which one? 1, 2, 3?")
- Thu: Seasonal ("Winter fit")
- Fri: Permission ("Your sign to cop this")

### Technical Specs

- All slides: 1080x1920 (9:16)
- Phone-shot only (63% outperforms studio)
- Trending audio required
- Caption: 2-5 words, native tone
- No text overlays on slides (or very minimal)
- Spark Ad link handles CTA — not the caption

## ABSOLUTE RULES

1. **Caption = 2-5 words. Sound like a friend, not a brand.**
2. **Slide 1 = full outfit on person. Never a product-only shot or text slide.**
3. **Phone-shot only. Never studio. 63% outperforms studio for purchases.**
4. **5-7 slides. Drop-off after 7.**
5. **Volume > perfection. 10-15 per week. Test, don't guess.**
6. **Post organic → boost winners. This IS the formula.**
7. **Trending audio always. Required for photo mode.**
8. **No spec text in captions. No '460 GSM'. No 'premium'. No 'limited'.**
