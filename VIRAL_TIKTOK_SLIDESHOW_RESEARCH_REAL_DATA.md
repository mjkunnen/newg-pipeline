## SECTION 6: BEST PRACTICES - WHAT THE DATA TELLS US

### Optimal Carousel Structure (Based on Top Performers)

1. **Slide count:** 5-10 slides (sweet spot for completion rate)
2. **First slide:** Must be a scroll-stopper (hook image, bold text, or curiosity gap)
3. **Audio:** ALWAYS use trending audio - can 10x reach via sound feed distribution
4. **Caption:** 200+ characters with relevant keywords
5. **Last slide:** Clear CTA (follow, save, shop link)
6. **Visual style:** Raw/authentic > polished/corporate
7. **Format:** Educational listicles are the #1 carousel format for saves

### Why Carousels Beat Video for Fashion Brands

1. **Higher save rate:** Viewers save carousels to revisit (outfit inspo, product details)
2. **Scalability:** Brands can produce carousels faster than video (Blume cited this specifically)
3. **Algorithm boost:** Swipe-through, pause, and re-read signals = high engagement = more distribution
4. **Repeat views:** Photo posts get consumed at viewer's own pace, increasing time-on-post
5. **Purchase intent:** Multiple angles/colors in one post drives higher purchase consideration
6. **Trust:** "Carousels don't scream 'ad,' even when they technically are"

## HONEST ASSESSMENT OF RESEARCH LIMITATIONS

1. **TikTok's architecture blocks external data scraping** - Individual carousel post metrics (views/likes/comments) are only fully visible within the TikTok app itself, not on external pages
2. **No single database exists** that catalogs "viral carousel posts" with slide-by-slide breakdowns
3. **Most industry articles cite trends and strategies** rather than specific post-level metrics
4. **Brand-specific carousel data is often paywalled** (e.g., CORQ Studio, Milk Karten newsletter)
5. **The carousel format is relatively new** (Photo Mode launched 2022) so historical case studies are limited compared to video format

### What Would Be Needed for Exact Post-Level Data:
- TikTok Creative Center access (ads.tiktok.com)
- Third-party analytics tools (Pentos, Exolyt, TokPortal)
- Direct monitoring of brand accounts within the TikTok app
- Brand partnership/creator program access
