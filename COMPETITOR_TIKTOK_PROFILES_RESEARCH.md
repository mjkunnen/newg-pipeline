## TIKTOK CAROUSEL/SLIDESHOW FORMAT INSIGHTS

### Platform Data (Official TikTok Stats)
- Carousel posts get **2.9x more comments** than video posts
- Carousel posts get **1.9x more likes** than video posts
- Carousel posts get **2.6x more shares** than video posts
- Carousels can contain **2-35 images** in scroll format

### Brand Case Studies Using Carousels

**Gymshark** (5M followers, 84.6M likes)
- Last 50 posts were EXCLUSIVELY photo carousels
- One carousel reached 5.6M views
- Performance same or higher than when posting videos
- Fully committed to carousel-only strategy

**Youthforia** (Skincare)
- Carousel posts see 11% higher reach than video
- Most popular post: 6.6M views (carousel format)
- Found greater international reach with carousels

**August** (Period care)
- Using photo slideshows since 2022
- Uses "reveal" mechanics, product photography, infographics
- Viral carousel about sales tax on menstrual products

**Bubble** (Skincare)
- Memes, educational material, day-in-the-life carousels
- "Allows users to digest content at their own pace"

### Carousel Strategy Types (7 Formats)
1. **"Where Is This Going?" Post** - Creates suspense/curiosity
2. **Themed Post** - Grouped around a topic/aesthetic
3. **Trend Post** - Riding current trends in carousel form
4. **One-Two Punch Post** - Setup then payoff
5. **Informative Post** - Educational/value content
6. **Instagram Post** - Repurposed IG content
7. **Inspirational Post** - Mood/lifestyle imagery

### Scalability Advantage
"With Carousels, brands are able to really pump them out at a higher rate... so there's a real scalability to them" - Key advantage over video content which requires more production.

## PAID/SPARK ADS INSIGHTS

### TikTok Spark Ads Performance
- **30% higher completion rate** than standard In-Feed ads
- **142% higher engagement rate** than In-Feed ads
- Boost organic posts (your own OR from creators with permission)
- Maintains all original engagement metrics (feels authentic)

### Fashion Nova's Proven Strategy
- Posts 5-10 organic TikToks daily
- Tracks which ones go viral
- Immediately boosts viral posts as Spark Ads
- Result: **40% lower CPA** compared to creating ads from scratch
- Pre-validated content = lower risk, higher performance

### Carousel Ad Specs
- Image resolutions: 1200x628 px (horizontal), 640x640 px (square), 720x1280 px (vertical)
- File size: Under 100KB per image
- Music required for carousels (enhances swipe experience)
- 2-35 swipeable images per ad

## RECOMMENDATIONS FOR NEWGARMENTS

Based on this competitor research:

1. **Follow the FiveLeafs model:** They're the closest comparable - Netherlands-based, similar size, TikTok-first, massive engagement from carousels. Their 31.8x likes-to-follower ratio proves the carousel format works for European streetwear.

2. **Go carousel-heavy like Gymshark:** Their switch to 50 consecutive carousel posts with maintained/improved performance proves the format works at scale.

3. **Use the Fashion Nova Spark Ads playbook:** Post multiple organic carousels daily, identify what goes viral, then boost winners with Spark Ads for 40% lower CPA.

4. **Leverage the 2.9x comment multiplier:** Carousels drive significantly more comments, which feeds the algorithm. Design slides that provoke opinions ("Which colorway?" / "Rate this fit 1-10").

5. **Emphasize Dutch/European identity:** FiveLeafs doesn't heavily push their Dutch origin - there's an opportunity to own the "Dutch streetwear" niche on TikTok.

6. **Production in Portugal angle:** Both FiveLeafs and potentially NewGarments use Portuguese production - this can be a trust/quality signal in carousel content.
