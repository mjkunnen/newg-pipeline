## 1. FIRST SLIDE — WHAT WORKS (Ranked by Performance)

### The Data

The first slide is BOTH your thumbnail AND your hook. On TikTok, 71.2% of purchases originate from feed discovery — meaning your first slide determines whether someone even sees the rest.

| First Slide Type | Performance | Why It Works |
|---|---|---|
| **Person wearing full outfit (fit check)** | Highest CTR + most native | People identify with people. Bypasses ad detection. Mirror selfies are the most native TikTok fashion format. |
| **Before/after or contrast reveal** | Highest swipe-through rate | Creates a curiosity gap. Viewer MUST swipe to see the payoff. |
| **Bold text on striking background** | Highest comment rate | "What do you want for your birthday?" — relatable text stops the scroll and starts a conversation. |
| **Moody/aesthetic lifestyle photo** | Highest save rate | Aspirational imagery triggers saves. Pinterest-board energy on TikTok. |
| **Product-only flat lay** | Lowest engagement | Looks like a catalog. Triggers ad detection. Only works for established brands with visual equity. |

### Key Finding: Person > Product (Always)

- **Lifestyle imagery + person wearing = up to 40% higher conversion** vs product-only shots (TikTok Shop data)
- **94% higher conversion** for high-quality vs low-quality photos
- **Phone-shot content: 63% chance of outperforming studio** for purchases (TikTok official)
- UGC-style first slides feel like creator content, not brand content = lower ad resistance

### The 5 First Slide Formats That Win

1. **Mirror Selfie / Fit Check** — Most native. Zero text needed. The outfit IS the hook. Used by: every top streetwear account.
2. **Bold Text + Question** — "What do you want for your birthday?" / "Rate this fit 1-10" / "Which one are you?" — Creates instant engagement. Used by: dialogue carousels, question formats.
3. **B&W Silhouette / Moody Shot** — Dark aesthetic = streetwear visual language. Stands out in a colorful feed. Used by: silhouette trend, dark aesthetic accounts.
4. **Candid / Walking Shot** — Movement creates energy. Feels like real life, not a photoshoot. Used by: Broken Planet, street-level accounts.
5. **Curated Aesthetic Image** — Mood board energy. Warm tones, clean composition. "More of this in 2026" text. Used by: quiet flex accounts, lifestyle carousels.

### What to AVOID as First Slide

- Product on white background (looks like Amazon listing)
- Logo or brand name slide (nobody cares yet)
- Text-heavy slide with specs (cognitive overload)
- Professional studio shot (triggers ad detection)
- Generic stock-style photo (no personality)

## 2. CAPTIONS — WHAT ACTUALLY PERFORMS IN STREETWEAR

### The Paradox: Simple vs Long

Research shows two competing findings:
- **TikTok's general data**: "Long captions get 3x more views on average"
- **Spark Ads data for fashion**: "Native captions (2-5 words) deliver 69% higher CVR and 37% lower CPA"

### The Resolution: Context Matters

| Context | Best Caption Style | Why |
|---|---|---|
| **Spark Ads (paid)** | 2-5 words, native tone | Caption is LOCKED. Simple = works in both organic and paid. Native = lower CPA. |
| **Organic (discovery)** | Short hook + hashtags for SEO | TikTok indexes captions for search. Keywords help discoverability. |
| **Save-focused content** | Utility promise in caption | "save for later", "your guide to..." triggers bookmark behavior. |
| **Comment-focused content** | Question or choice | "Which one? 1, 2, 3?" — lowest friction comment to type. |

### Top 15 Caption Styles for Streetwear Slideshows (By Category)

#### Category A: Identity Captions (Highest Virality)
These frame the product as WHO YOU ARE, not what the product IS.

1. **"School fit"** — #1 performer. Daily life framing.
2. **"Winter fit"** / **"Summer fit"** — Seasonal identity.
3. **"Fit check"** — Most native TikTok format.
4. **"Main character energy"** — Aspiration + identity.
5. **"The uniform"** — Positions the hoodie as essential.

**Why they work**: Processing fluency (Journal of Interactive Advertising) — simple, easy-to-process messages are perceived more positively. Nobody texts their friend "check out this 460 GSM hoodie." They say "yo this fit goes hard."

#### Category B: Save/Utility Captions (Highest Save Rate)
These trigger the bookmark reflex.

6. **"Let me save your fashion style"** — Helper positioning, not seller.
7. **"Save for later"** — Direct save CTA. #1 most-saved carousel format.
8. **"1 hoodie. 7 days."** — Utility promise. Viewers save to reference outfits later.

**Why they work**: Save rate above 3% = strong. Above 5% = exceptional algorithmic boost. Saves are the #1 algorithm signal because they indicate lasting value.

#### Category C: Engagement/Question Captions (Highest Comment Rate)
These make commenting effortless.

9. **"Which one are you? 1, 2, or 3?"** — Low-friction choice.
10. **"Would you wear this?"** — Binary yes/no = easiest comment.
11. **"Rate this fit 1-10"** — Gamified. Numbers = easy to type.
12. **"This or that?"** — Drives debate in comments.

**Why they work**: Carousels already get 2.9x more comments than video. Question captions amplify this. Every comment = more Spark Ad fuel.

#### Category D: Vibe/Emotion Captions (Highest Share Rate)
These feel personal, not promotional.

13. **"nothing.."** — Understated. Creates curiosity.
14. **"iykyk"** — Insider language. Exclusivity signal.
15. **"the vision"** / **"lately"** — Mood-based. Aesthetic energy.

**Why they work**: Ad blindness research shows 66% of consumers prefer friend/creator recommendations. These captions sound like a friend posted them, not a brand.

### Caption Rules (Non-Negotiable)

1. **NO product specs in caption** — "460 GSM" triggers ad detection
2. **NO marketing words** — "premium", "limited edition", "exclusive drop" = instant scroll
3. **NO long descriptions** — For Spark Ads, caption is locked. Keep it universal.
4. **USE native TikTok language** — Type how your audience texts
5. **Hashtags**: 3-5 focused tags, not 20 generic ones. TikTok indexes captions for SEO.

### NEWGARMENTS Own Data (Last 31 Posts)

|---|---|---|---|
| Avg Views | 326,117 | 73,718 | 4.4x |
| Avg Likes | 1,674 | 122 | 13.7x |
| Avg Comments | 22.3 | 1.3 | 17x |
| Avg Saves | 427 | 41.4 | 10.3x |

### Why Slideshows Win (Psychology)

1. **Swiping = high-intent engagement signal** — Physical swiping tells TikTok's algorithm the user is actively interested. Each swipe counts as an engagement event. Videos are passive; slideshows are active.

2. **Dwell time multiplier** — Average carousel session: 3 minutes. Users swipe through, go back, screenshot individual slides. More time on content = algorithm boost.

3. **Save behavior** — Slideshows are "reference content." People save them to come back to later (outfit inspo, gift ideas, styling guides). Save rate is TikTok's #1 distribution signal.

4. **Lower production barrier** — Phone photos > studio video. This means you can produce 10-15 variations per week vs 2-3 videos. Volume + testing is everything.

5. **Replay/re-swipe loop** — Users swipe through multiple times to catch details. Each pass = additional view/engagement.

## 5. OPTIMAL SLIDE COUNT & STRUCTURE

### Slide Count Performance

| Slides | Performance | Best For |
|---|---|---|
| **2 slides** | Highest completion rate (100% reach final) | Primadonna glow-up, before/after |
| **3-5 slides** | Sweet spot for engagement + saves | Fit checks, product reveals, dialogue |
| **5-7 slides** | Best for utility/save content | Weekly outfits, styling guides, wishlists |
| **7-10 slides** | Highest dwell time | Photo dumps, mood boards |
| **10+ slides** | Sharp drop-off after 7-10 | Only for deeply engaging story content |

### Target: 40-60% swipe-through rate for 8+ slide posts. Above 20% for any length.

### Universal Slide Structure

| Slide | Function | Rule |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | **STOP THE SCROLL** | Must work as thumbnail. Person wearing OR bold text hook. |
| 2-3 | **BUILD INTEREST** | Different angles, details, or story progression. |
| 4-5 | **DELIVER VALUE** | Alternative styling, colorways, or the "reveal." |
| Last | **DRIVE ACTION** | Best shot (save-worthy) or CTA context. End on desire. |

| Completion rate | +134% | baseline | Spark Ads |
| 6-sec view-through | +157% | baseline | Spark Ads |

### Why Spark Ads + Slideshows = Best Combination

1. Spark Ads inherit organic engagement (likes, comments, saves carry over)
2. Caption is locked = simple native captions work perfectly
3. Slideshows get higher organic engagement = better Spark Ad starting point
4. Lower creative production cost = more variations to test
5. Fashion brands see 40% lower CPA with organic → boost pipeline

### The Pipeline

1. Shoot 10-15 slideshow variations per week
2. Post organically with simple caption + trending sound
3. Monitor 24-48 hours: track saves, comments, shares
4. Boost top 2-3 as Spark Ads with conversion objective
5. Pause underperformers after 3-5 days (creative fatigue)
6. Repeat with fresh creative
